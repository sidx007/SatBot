# Director / Vice Chancellor

**Name:** Prof. Padmakumar Nair
**Position:** Director & Vice Chancellor
**Additional Role:** Dean, Thapar School of Liberal Arts & Sciences (TSLAS)

## Qualifications

- Ph.D. in Nano-structured Materials (Nanotechnology), University of Twente, The Netherlands
- Doctor of Engineering (Dr. Eng.) in Chemical Engineering, University of Tokyo, Japan
- MBA, Heriot-Watt University, UK
- M.Tech in Metallurgical Engineering, IIT Kharagpur, India

## Professional Experience

Dr. Nair brings over 30 years of combined experience in academia and industry, having worked across multiple countries including Netherlands, Denmark, USA, Japan, China, and South Korea. Before joining TIET, he served as Clinical Professor of Organization, Strategy and International Management at University of Texas at Dallas.

## Leadership at TIET

Under his leadership, TIET has:
- Achieved NIRF Rank 29 in Engineering (2024)
- Secured NAAC A++ accreditation
- Established the groundbreaking NVIDIA partnership
- Launched India's first campus-wide AI literacy program

## Research Focus

Specializes in leadership development, sustainability, strategizing for sustainability, social and commercial entrepreneurship, and nanomaterials. He has 55+ publications in international journals with 2600+ citations.
