## Role and Responsibilities

The Chancellor serves as the Chairman of the Board of Governors and provides strategic oversight for the institution. As the highest authority in the university's governance structure, the Chancellor ensures that TIET maintains its commitment to academic excellence, research innovation, and industry collaboration.

- Strategic partnerships including the landmark NVIDIA collaboration
- Maintaining NAAC A++ accreditation
