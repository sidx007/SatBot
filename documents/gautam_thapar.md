# Gautam Thapar

**Position:** President, Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology
**Role:** Member of the Founding Thapar Family
**Background:** Industrialist and Business Leader

## Legacy and Vision

Gautam Thapar represents the continuation of the Thapar family's legacy in education and industry. The Thapar Group was founded by Karam Chand Thapar, and the family has been instrumental in establishing and nurturing educational institutions in India.

## Contributions to TIET

- **Strategic Oversight:** Provides long-term vision and strategic direction
- **Industry Connections:** Leverages extensive business network for partnerships
- **Resource Mobilization:** Ensures adequate funding for institutional growth
- **Innovation Leadership:** Supports cutting-edge initiatives like AI integration

## Family Legacy

The institute was originally established in 1956 as Thapar Technology Campus under the vision of creating world-class technical education in India. Under the Thapar family's continued guidance, TIET has evolved into one of India's premier private engineering institutions.

## Current Initiatives

- Supporting the NVIDIA partnership for AI education
- Promoting sustainability and green campus initiatives
- Fostering entrepreneurship and innovation culture
- Enhancing international collaborations and rankings

## Vision Statement

"To maintain Thapar Institute's position as a global leader in engineering education, research, and innovation while staying true to the founding values of excellence, integrity, and social responsibility."
