# Department Heads and Associate Heads

## Overview

Each department at Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology is led by a Head of Department (HOD) and supported by Associate Heads who manage specific academic and administrative functions.

## Structure

### Engineering Departments
- **Computer Science & Engineering (CSE):** AI, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity
- **Electrical & Instrumentation Engineering (EIED):** Power systems, Control systems, Instrumentation
- **Mechanical Engineering (MED):** Design, Manufacturing, Thermal engineering
- **Civil Engineering:** Structural, Environmental, Transportation engineering
- **Chemical Engineering:** Process engineering, Biotechnology
- **Electronics & Communication Engineering:** VLSI, Communications, Signal processing

### Schools and Centers
- **Thapar School of Liberal Arts & Sciences (TSLAS):** Interdisciplinary programs
- **LM Thapar School of Management (LMTSM):** MBA, Executive programs
- **School of Mathematics:** Applied mathematics, Statistics
- **Thapar School of Advanced AI & Data Science (TSAAI):** AI, ML, Data Science

## Responsibilities

**Heads of Department:**
- Academic leadership and curriculum development
- Faculty management and recruitment
- Research coordination and funding
- Industry collaboration and partnerships
- Student academic matters

**Associate Heads:**
- Support HOD in administrative functions
- Coordinate specific programs (UG, PG, PhD)
- Manage departmental resources and facilities
- Liaise with other departments and administration
