# About KAIRA - Thapar's AI Assistant

**Name:** KAIRA (Knowledge-based AI Resource Assistant)
**Type:** AI-Powered Virtual Campus Assistant
**Launch:** Part of TIET's Digital Transformation Initiative (2024-25)

## Overview

KAIRA represents Thapar Institute's commitment to leveraging artificial intelligence for enhancing campus experience and administrative efficiency. Developed as part of the broader AI integration strategy following the NVIDIA partnership.

## Key Features

### Information Services
- **Academic Information:** Course details, schedules, examination dates, results
- **Admission Guidance:** Application procedures, eligibility criteria, important deadlines
- **Campus Navigation:** Building locations, facility information, maps
- **Student Services:** Hostel information, mess schedules, transportation

### AI Capabilities
- **Natural Language Processing:** Conversational interactions in multiple languages
- **24/7 Availability:** Round-the-clock assistance for students and faculty
- **Personalized Responses:** Context-aware answers based on user profile
- **Learning System:** Continuously improves through interaction feedback

### Technical Architecture
- **NVIDIA AI Integration:** Leverages NVIDIA DGX infrastructure
- **Database Connectivity:** Real-time access to institutional databases
- **Multi-platform Deployment:** Website, mobile app, social media integration
- **Security Features:** Data privacy and secure authentication

## Impact and Benefits

### For Students

### For Institution
