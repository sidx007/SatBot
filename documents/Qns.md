146**Where is the Synthetic Track?** The Synthetic Track for running and athletics is located next to the OAT (BAT) and the COS building.
147. **What is in Central Park?** Central Park is the green, wooded area in the middle of the campus. It's a great place to relax and contains the main Food Court, Kravings (TAN), and the Canteen.
148. **Where is the Canteen?** The main Canteen is located in the Central Park area, right next to the Food Court and Kravings (TAN).
149. **Where is G-Block?** G-Block is an academic building, home to the Civil Engineering department. You can find it in the main academic quad, near the Library and the Canteen. It also contains the eatery Ahaar.
150. **Where is the Shopping Complex?** The 'Shop Cmplx' (Shopping Complex) is located near the Main Gate, right next to the Guest House. You can find stationery, print shops, and other small stores there.
